Easy Examples of Laws : Traffic Laws

  • Traffic Lights

    You know those traffic lights on the road, right? They are like a game with rules. When the light is red, it means "Stop!" It's just like when you play a game, and someone says, "Freeze!" You have to stop moving. When the light turns green, it's like saying, "Go!" Just like in a game, when someone says, "Start moving!" you can go.

  • No Littering:

    Imagine you're in a park, and you have a candy wrapper. Instead of throwing it on the ground, there's a law that says you have to find a trash can and put it there. It's like a rule to keep our environment clean, similar to how you're told not to make a mess at home.
